Things to do

Excursions & Activities 🎯

Deep Creek is loaded — here's what we can get into while we're out there.

🌊

Adventure Sports Center

Whitewater rafting on a closed-loop course — go around as many times as you want. Great for groups and the reviews are RAVING. Full-body workout and full fun.

🔥 High Energy ⭐ 4.6 Water
🦅

All Earth Eco Tours

Kayaking on the Savage River Reservoir with bald eagle sightings. A 5.0 rated guide experience — perfect for the chill morning vibe before afternoon chaos.

✌️ Chill Vibes ⭐ 5.0 Nature
🎢

Wisp Mountain Park

Mountain coaster, scenic chairlift, mini golf, and ropes course. Great mix of thrills and laughs — perfect for a group day with a pass that covers multiple rides.

😄 Group Fun ⭐ 4.5 Adventure
🎱

Deep Creek Fun Zone

Go-karts, laser tag, bumper boats, mini golf, and arcade — all in one spot. Staff is great with large groups and there's a restaurant on site. Rain plan approved.

🌧️ Rain Plan ⭐ 4.3 Indoor/Out
